## Emphasis

```
*Italic text with asterisk*
_Italic text with underscore_

**Bold text with double asterisk**
__Italic text with double underscore__

***Bold Italic Text***
```

_Italic text with asterisk_
_Italic text with underscore_

**Bold text with double asterisk**
**Italic text with double underscore**

**_Bold Italic Text_**

## Lists

### Unordered

```
* Item 1
* Item 2
* Item 3
* Item 4
  * Item 4a
  * Item 4b
```

- Item 1
- Item 2
- Item 3
- Item 4
  - Item 4a
  - Item 4b

### Ordered

```
1. Item 1
1. Item 2
1. Item 3
    1. Item 3a
    1. Item 3b
```

1. Item 1
1. Item 2
1. Item 3
   1. Item 3a
   1. Item 3b
